Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (2)
  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Lay out one tortilla and put the chicken and cheese on it
  2. Put on low heat, and then put another tortilla on top
  3. Smash up avocado in the meantime
  4. After 6 mins, flip
  5. After 6 more mins, take off stove, cut, and put avocado on it
